## Overview

This project analyzes restaurant data from Yelp's Open Dataset to explore the factors that contribute to restaurant performance in Philadelphia.

The goal of this analysis was to understand the restaurant landscape by examining key business metrics such as customer ratings, review engagement, pricing, cuisine categories, and geographic distribution.

The final product is an interactive Tableau dashboard designed to help identify patterns within the Philadelphia restaurant market.

---

## Business Questions

This analysis aims to answer:

* What does the Philadelphia restaurant market look like?
* How are restaurants distributed geographically?
* Which cuisines are most common in the market?
* Does restaurant pricing influence customer ratings?
* How do customer ratings and review engagement vary across restaurants?

## Dataset

**Source:** Yelp Open Dataset

The dataset contains restaurant information including:

* Restaurant name
* Location
* Categories and cuisine types
* Star ratings
* Number of reviews
* Price range

The analysis focuses specifically on restaurants located in Philadelphia.

# Dashboard

## Philadelphia Restaurant Market Overview

The dashboard provides an overview of the Philadelphia restaurant industry through multiple visualizations:

### Key Performance Indicators

* Total number of restaurants analyzed
* Average restaurant rating
* Average review count

### Restaurant Map

Displays the geographic distribution of restaurants across Philadelphia, allowing users to explore restaurant concentration throughout the city.

### Price Analysis

Analyzes how restaurants are distributed across different price categories and compares performance between price levels.

### Cuisine Performance

Highlights the most common restaurant categories and compares average ratings across cuisines.

---

## Key Insights

Some of the main findings from the analysis include:

* Philadelphia has a diverse restaurant landscape with a wide range of cuisine categories represented.
* Restaurants average ratings show an upward trend in relation to restuarants price tier.
* Customer engagement varies significantly between restaurants, with review volume providing insight into restaurant popularity.
* Certain cuisine categories demonstrate stronger customer satisfaction compared to others.
